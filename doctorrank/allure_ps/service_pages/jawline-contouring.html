Non-Surgical Jawline Contouring Options

Jawline Filler Botox for Slimming Lower Face Contouring PDO Threads & Fat Transfer

Jawline Filler (Hyaluronic Acid)

Result Last: 12-18 months | Downtime: Minimal

Instantly adds volume and definition for a stronger jawline. Results last about 12–18 months with minimal downtime. We use safe hyaluronic acid fillers and ensure the enhancement looks natural (never overdone).

Botox for Jawline Slimming

Result Last: 4-6 months | Downtime: None

Relaxes overactive masseter (jaw) muscles to slim a wide or square jawline. Botox injections into the jaw muscle take only a few minutes and require no downtime. This approach is essentially a non-surgical jawline reduction – a great alternative to jawline reduction surgery in NYC for those with a bulky jaw muscle.

Lower Face Contouring with Fillers

Result Last: 12-18 months | Downtime: Minimal

Balances the chin, jaw, and even cheeks using dermal fillers for overall facial harmony. Sometimes a chin filler NYC treatment is combined with jawline filler to ensure the chin and jawline are proportionate.

PDO Thread Lifts & Fat Transfer

Result Last: 1-2 years | Downtime: Minimal

These minimally invasive options can tighten and add volume to the jawline without traditional surgery. PDO threads can lift sagging skin along the jaw (results last around 1–2 years). Fat transfer uses your own fat to add volume to a weak jawline.

Surgical Jawline Contouring Options

Jawline Implants Chin Augmentation Jawline Lift

Jawline Implants

Result Last: Permanent

Customized implants provide permanent structural enhancement for a stronger, more chiseled jawline. For a very weak jaw structure, Dr. Heller may recommend a custom implant along the jaw (placed through a small incision inside the mouth, so there are no visible scars).

Chin Augmentation (Implant or Sliding Genioplasty)

Result Last: Permanent

Improves a weak or receding chin to balance your profile. A chin implant can be inserted via a discreet incision under the chin or inside the mouth, adding forward projection. Alternatively, a sliding genioplasty repositions your own chin bone.

Neck Liposuction & Lower Facelift (Jawline Lift)

Result Last: Long-lasting

Removes excess fat under the chin and tightens loose skin for a sharper jaw-neck angle. A lower (mini) facelift focuses on lifting the jawline and jowls, often performed under local anesthesia in about 2–3 hours.

Jawline Contouring FAQs

How do I know if I need jawline surgery or filler?

Dr. Heller will assess your jawline and skin during a consultation to determine whether a non-surgical approach (like filler or Botox) or surgery is best. Minor enhancements can often be done with fillers, whereas more significant changes or loose skin usually require a surgical solution.

How long do jawline fillers last?

Jawline filler results typically last about 12–18 months. Many patients get a yearly touch-up to maintain definition. Repeated filler treatments can also have a cumulative effect by stimulating collagen, which means you may see some longer-lasting improvement over time.

Is jawline contouring surgery permanent?

Yes. Surgical jawline enhancements are intended to be long-lasting or permanent. A chin or jaw implant provides a new structure that will not dissolve, and a surgical lift or genioplasty repositions tissue permanently. Of course, your face will continue to age naturally. By contrast, minimally invasive thread lifts are not permanent. Their results last about 1–2 years, though repeating the procedure can prolong the benefits.

Is there downtime with jawline filler or Botox?

Very little. You might have mild swelling or bruising at the injection sites, but most patients return to normal activities, even work, within a day. We'll provide tips to minimize any swelling and help you heal quickly.

Can jawline filler and surgery be combined?

Yes. In some cases, we combine treatments for the best result. For example, a patient might undergo surgical chin augmentation for structure and later add a bit of filler along the jawline for fine-tuning. Dr. Heller will guide you on the ideal combination if a multi-step approach is appropriate for you.
