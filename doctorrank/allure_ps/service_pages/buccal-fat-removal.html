What is Buccal Fat Removal

Buccal fat removal (also known as buccal fat pad removal or buccal fat reduction) is a minor surgical procedure that removes excess fat pads from the lower cheeks to slim your face and enhance your cheekbones. Dr. Elliot Heller performs the surgery through a small incision inside the mouth, so there are no visible scars.

The procedure takes about one hour under local anesthesia and is completed in a single session. Using precise, gentle techniques, Dr. Heller removes the buccal fat pad to create a thinner, more contoured mid-face.

What to Expect: Procedure & Recovery

A comprehensive overview of your buccal fat removal journey from procedure to full recovery

Procedure

Buccal fat removal is an outpatient procedure. Under local anesthesia, Dr. Heller makes a small incision inside each cheek and carefully removes the buccal fat pad. He takes out just the right amount to slim your face while preserving your natural contours. The incisions are then closed with dissolvable stitches. The surgery takes about one hour, and you'll be able to go home the same day.

Recovery Timeline

Healing is typically quick and comfortable. Expect some cheek swelling for a few days (mostly gone by about 1â€“2 weeks). Any minor bruising will fade quickly. You can usually return to work or school within 1â€“2 days. Many patients feel fine the next day.

By around 2 months post-op, the swelling is fully gone, and your final results are visible. You'll notice a slimmer face and more defined cheekbones that are permanent.
