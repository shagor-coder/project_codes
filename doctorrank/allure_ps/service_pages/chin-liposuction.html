What is Chin Liposuction

Chin liposuction (submental liposuction) is a specialized procedure to remove excess fat under the chin and sculpt a sharper jawline. It's an effective double chin removal method for patients who struggle with fullness beneath the chin despite diet and exercise.

During the procedure, Dr. Elliot Heller uses a tiny cannula (thin tube) to gently suction out unwanted fat through small, hidden incisions. The result is a smoother neck contour and improved facial profile often described as a subtle 'jawline lift'.

Because it directly removes fat cells, the improvement is typically permanent; fat won't return to that area as long as you maintain your weight. Dr. Heller performs chin liposuction using advanced techniques, including laser liposuction (SmartLipo) and even Renuvion® J-Plasma (helium plasma) for skin tightening to ensure natural-looking results with minimal trauma.

As a certified surgeon with over 20 years of experience, Dr. Heller prides himself on achieving a contoured jawline that still looks like you, only more defined.

Are You a Candidate for Chin Liposuction?

Chin liposuction is ideal for men and women who have stubborn fat under the chin or along the upper neck that creates the appearance of a double chin or soft jawline. Good candidates typically:

Double Chin Concerns

Have a noticeable double chin or fullness under the chin that they wish to eliminate for a better jawline contour.

Healthy Weight

Are at a healthy, stable weight. Chin lipo is best for spot fat reduction; it's not a weight loss method. The ideal patient is close to their ideal body weight, perhaps with just a small pocket of fat that won't budge.

Good Skin Elasticity

Have good skin elasticity. This procedure relies on the skin's ability to retract once the fat is removed. Patients in their 20s-40s usually have good elasticity, but Dr. Heller will assess your skin.

Permanent Solution

Prefer a permanent fat removal solution with quick results. If you want to avoid multiple sessions of injections or freezing treatments, chin lipo's one-and-done approach is attractive.

General Health

Are non-smokers (or willing to quit before surgery) and in general good health, to support proper healing.

How the Chin Liposuction Procedure Works

Chin liposuction is typically performed as an outpatient procedure and usually takes under an hour. This quick, safe procedure can dramatically improve your profile.

Local Anesthesia

Dr. Heller uses local anesthesia with sedation, so you'll be awake but comfortable (general anesthesia is rarely needed).

Tiny Incision

He makes a tiny incision under the chin (or sometimes just behind the earlobe) and inserts a thin cannula to gently suction out the unwanted fat.

Advanced Technology

Using precise, controlled movements, Dr. Heller sculpts a smoother neck. He can also use laser liposuction (SmartLipo) or Renuvion (J-Plasma) device for skin tightening.

Quick Closure

Once the desired contour is achieved, the small incision is closed with a bandage. The incision is only a few millimeters, so any scar is minimal and hidden.

Recovery and Results: What to Expect

Recovery from chin liposuction is generally quick and comfortable. You will go home the same day and receive detailed aftercare instructions from Dr. Elliot Heller.

Compression Garment

(First few days)

You'll wear a chin strap bandage for the first few days (and at night for about a week) to reduce swelling and support your new chin contour.

  • Reduces swelling effectively
  • Supports healing tissues
  • Comfortable to wear

Minimal Downtime

(24 hours)

Most patients return to work and light activities after 24 hours. Avoid strenuous exercise for about 1–2 weeks.

  • Back to work quickly
  • Light activities allowed
  • Exercise restriction temporary

Swelling & Bruising

(1-2 weeks)

Expect swelling for a few days and possibly mild bruising for up to a week or two. Any bruising is usually minor and clears up within 1–2 weeks.

  • Swelling subsides quickly
  • Minimal bruising
  • Natural healing process

Final Results

(30 days)

You'll see improvement once initial swelling goes down (around one week). Your jawline will continue to refine over the next 2–3 weeks as swelling subsides.

  • Gradual improvement
  • Refined jawline
  • Long-lasting results

We use laser liposuction, which removes fat and tightens the skin at the same time. This avoids the common issue of a skinny, loose neck after fat removal, says Dr. Heller. By combining fat removal and skin tightening, he ensures patients leave with a sculpted and youthful profile.
